<div class="lang-en">

The hotkey is what wakes TypeLate up. Pick a key you can press without moving your hands away from the keyboard, and pick a trigger mode that matches how you talk.

</div>
<div class="lang-zh">

熱鍵是叫醒 TypeLate 的按鍵。選一個不用離開鍵盤就能按到的鍵，搭配最像你說話節奏的觸發模式。

</div>

## Choose your key

<div class="lang-en">

Open **Settings → Hotkeys & Input → Hotkey**. Defaults differ by platform:

- **macOS default**: <Kbd keys="Fn" /> — already on every Apple keyboard, no conflict with text input.
- **Windows default**: <Kbd keys="Right Alt" /> — clear of typical text shortcuts.

Other presets you can pick:

- <Kbd keys="⌥" /> (Option / Alt) — left or right.
- <Kbd keys="⌃" /> (Control) — left or right.
- <Kbd keys="⌘" /> (Command).
- <Kbd keys="⇧" /> (Shift) — works but easy to trigger accidentally; pair with Double-Tap mode.

Click **Custom key** to record any other key. TypeLate captures the next keydown — press once and confirm. <Kbd keys="Esc" /> is reserved (used to abort recordings) and rejected with an error. Keys that conflict with common OS shortcuts (e.g. <Kbd keys={["⌘", "Q"]} />) are warned but not blocked.

</div>
<div class="lang-zh">

開啟 **設定 → 熱鍵與輸入 → 熱鍵**。預設值依平台不同：

- **macOS 預設**：<Kbd keys="Fn" /> — Apple 鍵盤都有，不會跟文字輸入衝突。
- **Windows 預設**：<Kbd keys="Right Alt" /> — 不會撞到常見文字快捷鍵。

其他可選預設：

- <Kbd keys="⌥" />（Option / Alt）— 左或右。
- <Kbd keys="⌃" />（Control）— 左或右。
- <Kbd keys="⌘" />（Command）。
- <Kbd keys="⇧" />（Shift）— 可用，但很容易誤觸，建議搭 Double-Tap 模式。

點 **自訂按鍵** 可錄製任何其他鍵。TypeLate 會捕捉下一次按鍵 — 按一下確認即可。<Kbd keys="Esc" /> 為保留鍵（用來中止錄音），會被拒絕並提示。會撞到常見系統快捷鍵的按鍵（例如 <Kbd keys={["⌘", "Q"]} />）會給警告但不會擋。

</div>

<Callout type="warn" title="Accessibility permission" titleZh="輔助使用權限">

<div class="lang-en">

Global hotkey capture requires the **Accessibility** permission on macOS. If your hotkey doesn't fire, open **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility** and confirm TypeLate is in the list and toggled on. After app updates, you may need to remove and re-add the entry — macOS caches Accessibility against the code signature.

</div>
<div class="lang-zh">

全域熱鍵需要 macOS 的 **輔助使用** 權限。如果熱鍵沒反應，請到 **系統設定 → 隱私權與安全性 → 輔助使用** 確認 TypeLate 在列表內並已啟用。App 更新後可能需要先移除再重新加入 — macOS 會以程式簽章為快取依據。

</div>
</Callout>

### Hold

<div class="lang-en">

- **Press and hold** the hotkey to record. **Release** to stop and process.
- Best for short utterances; you don't need to manage a state.
- Default for users who like push-to-talk feel (think walkie-talkie).

</div>
<div class="lang-zh">

- **按住** 熱鍵錄音，**放開** 停止並開始處理。
- 適合短句，不用管「目前是不是在錄」。
- 喜歡 push-to-talk 手感（像對講機）的人首選。

</div>

### Toggle

<div class="lang-en">

- **Press once** to start. **Press again** to stop.
- Hands-free friendly — no need to keep a finger down while you think.
- Comes with a **5-minute safety timeout** that auto-stops recording if you forget.
- Default in onboarding.

</div>
<div class="lang-zh">

- **按一下** 開始，**再按一下** 結束。
- 免手按，思考時手指可以離開鍵盤。
- 內建 **5 分鐘自動停止** 防呆，忘了停會自動切。
- Onboarding 流程預設此模式。

</div>

### Double-Tap

<div class="lang-en">

- **Tap the key twice quickly** (within 300 ms) to start. Tap twice again to stop.
- Prevents accidental triggers on keys you also use for typing (e.g. <Kbd keys="⇧" /> or <Kbd keys="⌃" />).
- Slightly more friction; recommended only when you can't avoid sharing a key with normal input.

</div>
<div class="lang-zh">

- **快速雙擊**（300 毫秒內）開始錄音，再雙擊一次結束。
- 避免在會打字的按鍵上誤觸（例如 <Kbd keys="⇧" /> 或 <Kbd keys="⌃" />）。
- 多一個動作，建議只在無法避免共用按鍵時使用。

</div>

## Tips

<Callout type="tip" title="Right-side modifiers" titleZh="善用右側修飾鍵">

<div class="lang-en">

If you bind to <Kbd keys="⌥" /> or <Kbd keys="⌃" />, choosing the **right-side** variant prevents collisions with typical left-hand shortcuts (<Kbd keys={["⌃", "C"]} />, <Kbd keys={["⌥", "←"]} />). Both sides are listed separately in the preset menu.

</div>
<div class="lang-zh">

綁定 <Kbd keys="⌥" /> 或 <Kbd keys="⌃" /> 時，選 **右側** 變體可以避開左手常用的快捷鍵（例如 <Kbd keys={["⌃", "C"]} />、<Kbd keys={["⌥", "←"]} />）。預設選單把左右兩側分開列出。

</div>
</Callout>

<Callout type="info" title="Silence detection" titleZh="靜音偵測">

<div class="lang-en">

If TypeLate detects no speech for the first second of a recording, the HUD shows a hint. This is a heads-up that the mic might be muted or the wrong device is selected — see [Audio settings](/docs/getting-started#5-your-first-transcription).

</div>
<div class="lang-zh">

開始錄音的第一秒若沒偵測到語音，HUD 會跳提示。通常是麥克風被靜音或裝置選錯 — 可參考 [音訊設定](/docs/getting-started#5-your-first-transcription)。

</div>
</Callout>
